About Me

My name is Jordan Masone. I am twenty-two years old and was born in Long Island, New York. I spent a small amount of time in Jamaica when I was younger but moved back to the U.S. when I turned seven. My family and I relocated to Orlando, Florida when we moved back to the states. I attended West Orange High School in Winter Garden, Florida and graduated from their in 2016. I then attended Florida State University shortly thereafter and pursued a bachelors degree in music education. I graduated with my bachelors degree in December of 2020. I am currently studying at UCF to earn a certification in Web Development. I hope to be out in the field by the end of June, 2021.

My previous work positions include, Panda Express, Moe's Southwest Grill, Marching Band Tech, LifeGuard, and Stage Operator. I will also be adding subsitute teacher to the list sometime shortly. If you need to learn more about me, please feel free to contact me.
